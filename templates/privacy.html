Privacy Policy

I value your privacy. This page explains what information I collect when you visit my website and how that information is handled.

Information Collected

When you interact with this website, specifically through the Contact Form, the following data is collected:

  • Personal Information: Your name and email address (only if you choose to provide them).
  • Metadata: Your IP address and browser user-agent string.

Why This Data is Collected

I collect this limited information for the following reasons:

  • Spam Prevention: To block malicious bots and automated submissions.
  • Security: To protect the website from cyberattacks or unauthorized access.
  • Compatibility: The user-agent helps me understand which browsers my visitors use so I can ensure the site displays correctly.

Data Storage and Retention

  • Storage: Data submitted via the contact form is stored securely to facilitate communication.
  • Retention: I only keep your contact information for as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry. Technical data (IP/User-agent) is typically deleted or anonymized after a short period.

Third-Party Sharing

I do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your data to outside parties. I may only share information if required by law or to protect the safety and rights of myself or others.

Cookies

This website does not currently use cookies for tracking purposes. If this changes in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the data I have about you.
  • Request that I delete your personal information.
  • Withdraw consent for me to contact you.

Contact Information

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, you can reach me via the contact form on this website.
